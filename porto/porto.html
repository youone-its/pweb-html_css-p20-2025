Projects

Aquatro

Aquatro is a solar-powered flood detection system designed to improve safety and awareness in flood-prone areas. This project combines water level sensors with an automatic lighting mechanism that activates whenever the system detects rising water levels. By using renewable solar energy, Aquatro operates sustainably without relying on external power sources. The activating light serves as an early warning indicator, helping communities and individuals to take quick action during emergencies. This project aims to provide a low-cost, eco-friendly, and reliable solution to minimize risks caused by floods.
